Why MHRS?

Goals and Objectives.

The MHRS(मानवा हित रक्षा संगठन) holds true integrity and trust and faith in the Constitution of India. With the inspiration from the ideals of the Father of the Nation, freedom fighter Mahatma Gandhi, Constitutionalist Dr. Bhimrao Ambedkar, Mother Teresa, former judge P.N. Bhagwati, the MHRS(मानवा हित रक्षा संगठन), all rights such as human rights, child rights, women's rights, which are described in the Constitution, include democracy, secularism and Believes in socialism. The belief of the MHRS(मानवा हित रक्षा संगठन) is in such a system in which the general public can be aware, propagate education, peace and prosperity with their rights.The MHRS(मानवा हित रक्षा संगठन) will maintain true faith and integrity to the Constitution of India established by the law and will keep hostility towards the principles of embedded human rights, socialism, secularism and social justice. MHRS(मानवा हित रक्षा संगठन) will maintain India's sovereignty, unity and integrity as integral.

Why MHRS?

Our Vision.

  1. To make citizens aware of their rights and protect their dignity.
  2. Make citizens aware of all constitutional rights of citizens.
  3. To cooperate with governance, administration and police to cooperate with them in preventing corruption and crimes and to bring justice to right person.
  4. Develop social ethical, character and intellectual, spiritual development and development of hate and ideological harmony among the masses
  5. Try to stop crime and exploitation by establishing cooperation with the administration.
  6. Environment saftey.
